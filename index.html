ABOUT ME

I am 23 years old and I am currently completing studies in computer science techonology with a profile in video game programming at LaSalle College in Montreal. I have lived in Montreal my whole life and have had several part-time jobs throughout my studies that have helped shape me into the hard working and social individual I am today.

My goals are to one day work at a game studio where they create AAA titles. This does not have to necessarily be a studio in Montreal as I have always wanted to experience the excitement of moving to somewhere new. While I would find it hard leaving my friends and family, I believe it would be an extremely important experience in my life.
