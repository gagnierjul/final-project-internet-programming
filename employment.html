Bell Canada

I worked as a field technician for Bell Canada for about two years. My job was to reach customer's houses and install or repair Bell products such as; fiber optic internet, copper internet, television or phone lines. I was provided with all the tools as well as a company truck to use during my working hours.

This was very good experience not only in understanding the telecommunications infrastructure, but also for customer service. It was a very unusual job for a student, but the funny thing is I found this job at a CEGEP carreer fair.
